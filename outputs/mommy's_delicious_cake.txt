Mommy's Delicious Cake
Mommy was in the kitchen baking a yummy cake. She carefully mixed the ingredients together and put it in the oven. While it was baking, she decorated the kitchen with colorful balloons and ribbons. After an hour, Mommy took out her delicious cake from the oven. She topped it with colorful sprinkles to make it extra special. Everyone in the family could not wait to have a bite of Mommy's delicious cake. They all sang happy birthday and blew out the candles together. Everyone enjoyed eating the cake and they loved how tasty it was! Mommy felt so proud about making such a delicious treat for her family.
