Alex's Art Adventure
Once upon a time, there was a little boy named Alex who loved to paint. One day, he decided to create something extra special. With a brush and some vibrant colors, Alex went to work and created the most beautiful painting ever seen! He used shades of blue, green, yellow and orange to make a one-of-a-kind masterpiece. After admiring his creation for some time, Alex proudly hung it up on the wall for everyone to enjoy. From then on, every day was an art adventure for Alex!
